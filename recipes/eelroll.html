EEL ROLL

DESCRIPTION

A testament to the artistry of the Japanese cuisine that combines the delicate and rich flavours of fresh eel with a harmonious blend of complementary ingredients

INGREDIENTS

  • Eel
  • Seaweed Paper
  • Sticky Rice
  • Avocado
  • Cucumber

STEPS

  1. Put rice on seaweed paper and spread it evenly
  2. Place all the good stuff at one end of paper and rice
  3. ROLL IT UP! AND SLICE ACCORDINGLY OR JUST BITE INTO IT
